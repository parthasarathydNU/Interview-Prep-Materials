1 byte = 8 bits
1 Kilo Byte = 1024 Bytes ( 10 ^ 3 ) Bytes
1 Mega Byte = ( 10 ^ 6 ) Bytes

| 1 Bytes | 8 Bits          |
| ------- | --------------- |
| 1 KB    | ~ 10 ^ 3 Bytes  |
| 1 MB    | ~ 10 ^ 6 Bytes  |
| 1 GB    | ~ 10 ^ 9 Bytes  |
| 1 TB    | ~ 10 ^ 12 Bytes |
| 1 PB    | ~ 10 ^ 15 Bytes |
| 1 EB    | ~ 10 ^ 18 Bytes |
